# Value Proposition Template

A fill-in-the-blanks template for articulating *why* HIL matters for your
product, your team, and your organization — before the conversation
turns to dollars.

## What it helps you answer

- Who is the audience for this proposal? (engineering leadership, finance,
  an executive sponsor)
- What problem does HIL solve for *them* specifically?
- What is the cost of doing nothing?
- What does success look like in 6 months, 12 months, and 24 months?
- How does this fit the broader product and quality strategy?
