# Supporting Materials

Reference data, worked examples, and citations that back up the numbers
in the NPV, ROI, and value-proposition templates.

Use this folder as the answer to "where did that number come from?"

## Likely contents

- Industry benchmarks and citations
- Worked examples from real (anonymized) HIL programs
- Glossary of business-case terms (NPV, IRR, payback, hurdle rate)
- Pitfalls and counter-arguments to anticipate

Materials will be added here as the session approaches.
